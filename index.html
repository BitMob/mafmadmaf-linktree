mafmadmaf

Guangzhou-based artist Maf is also an art director and a performing musician under the name 'mafmadmaf'. As a minimalist in expression, he writes atmosphere-based, delicate yet sometimes fierce electronic music for modular synthesizers, exploring the vibrant sonic textures. He also focuses on but is not limited to, noise, experimentation, and soundscapes. He has performed at several experimental music events such as the Tokyo Festival of Modular and the Modular Commune.

植根于中国廣州的合成器電子音樂人 mafmadmaf，也是一個美術指導，極簡主義者。他書寫以氛圍為基調的合成器電子樂，音色細膩時而凶猛、充滿生機；專注於噪聲、實驗和聲音景觀，表達時空觀念，但不限於此。以模塊化合成器為核心樂器，常把它們帶入自然環境中演奏。

在摩登天空和環形山等廠牌發佈作品。參加東京模塊音樂節（Tokyo Festival of Modular）和模塊大會（Modular Commune）等交流活動。
